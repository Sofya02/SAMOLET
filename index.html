Mind Tables

The My Profile page is the personal page of an employee of the intracorporate platform. It is similar to the profile of a social network that describes competencies and interests. Platform storing employee documents.

MAIN TASK

In the context of remote work for most of the company, it is important for the company's productive work to provide the most detailed portrait of an employee.

Research

Together with the product manager, we conducted a study in which we identified two main types of target audience.
We conducted several interviews with users of the old platform and ifound out the main problems.

Job To Be Done

This second step of the research

When I need to find an employee's mail, I want to see a link on the main page of his profile

When searching for an employee, I want to search by photo and I’d like to see a large avatar

I need to understand when colleagues start / finish work from a different time zone

When forming a team, I need to select an employee according to their skills

For greater attractiveness of the portal, the profile should be similar to a social network

When plan work / vacation, I want to understand how long they may be

When solving new problems, I want to know who I can ask for help depending on the problem

I‘d like to find employees with common interests outside office

Before the meeting, I want to learn more about colleagues I don't know yet
